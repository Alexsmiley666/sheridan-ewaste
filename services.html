Residential Pickups

We help households safely dispose of unwanted electronics. Whether you're clearing out a closet or replacing old devices, we'll pick up items like:

  • Laptops and tablets
  • Phones and chargers
  • TVs and monitors
  • Printers and small appliances
  • Gaming systems, routers, cables, and more

Commercial Services

We work with local businesses, schools, and offices to recycle larger volumes of equipment. We also offer optional data destruction certificates upon request. Common pickups include:

  • Bulk computers and peripherals
  • Office phones and telecom equipment
  • Networking hardware and server components
  • Point-of-sale (POS) systems
  • Copiers, shredders, and electronics accessories

Why It Matters

We believe recycling should be easy and secure for everyone. By serving both residential and commercial clients, we're helping Sheridan reduce waste and keep valuable materials out of the landfill. Plus, our free pickup and responsible disassembly makes it effortless to do the right thing.
