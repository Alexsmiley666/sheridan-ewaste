Our Process

How we reuse, repair, and recycle electronics to reduce e-waste in Sheridan, WY.

Home Our Process Who We Serve Why Reuse Schedule Pickup

Assessment & Sorting

Every item is carefully evaluated. If it powers on or can be refurbished, we keep it out of the landfill. Otherwise, we determine the best way to harvest useful parts.

Refurbishing

Working or fixable items are repaired, cleaned, and made ready for reuse. These go to new homes, supporting sustainability and helping the community access affordable tech.

Salvaging & Recycling

Devices that cannot be reused are disassembled and separated by material. Metals, plastics, and boards are sent to certified recyclers, ensuring minimal waste and maximum resource recovery.
