How It Works

We start by inspecting all electronics we receive. If something can be reused or refurbished, we do it. If not, we break it down carefully to salvage working parts and reduce waste.

Functional devices are cleaned and repaired for reuse. We harvest valuable components like RAM, hard drives, and screens to help save other electronics from being discarded. This keeps useful items out of landfills and puts them into the hands of people who need them.

Anything that cannot be reused is separated into recyclable materials and sent to certified recycling partners. By breaking down each item with care, we ensure the most efficient recovery of metals, plastics, and boards.
