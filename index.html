Free Electronics Recycling in Sheridan, WY

We’re a local, eco-conscious e-waste recycling service offering free electronics pickup for both residents and businesses. From laptops and phones to appliances and printers, we accept it all — and ensure it’s handled responsibly. By refurbishing, harvesting parts, or recycling efficiently, we keep valuable resources out of landfills and put working tech back in the hands of those who need it.
