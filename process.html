Our Process

How we reuse, repair, and recycle electronics to reduce e-waste in Sheridan, WY.

Home Our Process Who We Serve Why Reuse Schedule Pickup

Step 1: Inspection & Testing

Every device we receive is inspected and tested to determine if it can be reused, repaired, or harvested for parts. This helps us decide whether to refurbish or disassemble the item.

Step 2: Refurbishing if Possible

If an item is fixable, we clean and repair it for reuse. This helps give it a second life and keeps it out of the landfill — benefiting local families, schools, and nonprofits.

Step 3: Disassembly & Part Harvesting

Non-working devices are carefully taken apart. We save components like RAM, hard drives, screens, power supplies, and fans to repair other devices or sell locally to repair techs and hobbyists.

Step 4: Component Reuse

Working parts are either reused in our refurbishing efforts or resold to individuals or organizations that can use them. This helps extend the life of existing devices and reduces waste.

Step 5: Efficient Recycling

Any materials that can’t be reused are sorted — plastics, metal, and boards — and sent to certified recycling centers for proper handling. This maximizes material recovery and minimizes landfill waste.
